Mikey Elmers

I am a PhD researcher for the PINTS project which focuses on pause-internal phonetic particles in speech communication. Within that project, I examine the influence of pause particles in speech synthesis. My supervisors are Prof. Dr. Bernd Möbius and Dr. Jürgen Trouvain. I am part of the Language Science and Technology department at Saarland University, Saarbrücken, Germany. My interests are in phonetics, speech synthesis, and memory. My work involves experimental design, data collection, statistical analyses, coding, data visualization, and the promotion of reproducible research practices.
